Sky, the Energetic Adventurer

Sky was designed as an exploration bot, built for mapping uncharted lands and tackling new frontiers. Their enthusiasm for discovery is unmatched, and they’re always planning the next exciting adventure. With an endless supply of energy and curiosity, Sky inspires the team to think outside the box and take risks when the path isn’t clear.

Connection: Sky found the group during a field mission, where they were gathering data on teamwork and communication. After realizing the group was full of creative individuals, Sky decided to embark on their adventures together.
